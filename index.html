Bathtub — No Room for Emo

Two brothers. One battlefield. Zero chill. This is the TMNT Commander deck that turns the sewer into a war zone — token armies backed by Raphael's rage, disciplined by Leonardo's vision. Tokens on tokens. Counters on counters. Every pizza slice earned in blood. Cowabunga, motherf***er.

Why This Deck Slaps
🐢

Turtle Power Tokens

Doubling Season + Parallel Lives means every token trigger doubles, then doubles again. Drop a Raphael swing and watch the board flood with Turtle tokens that hit like a truck.

⚡

Dark Ritual Explosivity

Dark Ritual + Food Chain gives you degenerate mana on turn 2. This isn't a slow deck. This is "I win before you realize what happened" energy backed by Underworld Breach recursion.

🍕

Technique Stacking

Raphael's Technique, Donatello's Technique, and New Generation's Technique stack into a value engine so disgusting it'd make Shredder rage-quit. Train your turtles. Win the game.

🔄

Infinite Recursion Lines

Teleportation Circle + Sword of Hearth and Home keeps your best ETB triggers firing every turn. Yuriko in the 99 means every Ninjutsu swing is a free info-gather and a gut punch.

💀

The Ooze Wins

Mutagen Man + The Ooze + Agatha's Soul Cauldron creates a counter-accumulation loop that turns any creature into an Ooze-powered monstrosity. Science as a weapon.

🎮

High Score, Always

Arcade Cabinet, High Score, and Level Up give this deck a genuine video game soul — literal +1/+1 counters as points. Win the game like you're clearing the final stage.
